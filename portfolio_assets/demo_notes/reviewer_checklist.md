# Reviewer Demo Checklist

Use this checklist when presenting Strategic Intelligence Decision Companion as a portfolio product.

## Five-Minute Review

1. Open the README and read the opening product narrative.
2. Review the Decision Workspace screenshot placeholder.
3. Open [Demo Walkthrough](../../docs/DemoWalkthrough.md).
4. Inspect the V4 workspace demo under `demo_case_outputs/v4_workspace/`.
5. Open one generated brief and one JSON artifact.
6. Review [Enterprise Readiness Checklist](../../docs/EnterpriseReadinessChecklist.md).

## What To Look For

- The product is a Decision Intelligence workspace, not a chatbot.
- Evidence is reviewable and traceable.
- Decision pathways are drafts, not recommendations.
- Reviewer notes and unresolved questions remain human-controlled.
- Outputs are local artifacts that can be inspected.

## What Not To Claim

- Do not claim autonomous research.
- Do not claim legal, investment, or compliance advice.
- Do not claim prediction or forecasting.
- Do not claim production SaaS readiness.
