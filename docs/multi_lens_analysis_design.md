# Multi-Lens Analysis Design

## Supported Lenses

- Economics.
- Political Economy.
- International Relations.
- Legislative / Regulatory.
- Business Strategy.

## Output Fields

Each lens generates:

- Hypothesis.
- Supporting observations.
- Limitations.
- Evidence references.

## Design Rationale

Different disciplines notice different mechanisms. A business strategy lens may
emphasize resilience and positioning. An international relations lens may
emphasize cross-border strategic behavior. A regulatory lens may emphasize
implementation and compliance obligations.

The system presents these as interpretations, not conclusions.
