# Business Analytics Relevance

Strategic Intelligence Agent is not just an AI demo. It is an analytics product
that turns unstructured source material into a repeatable decision-support
workflow.

## Information Extraction

The system extracts issues, actors, regions, industries, policy terms, and
document type guesses. This converts unstructured text into structured fields
that can be reviewed and reused.

## Retrieval

The system retrieves two kinds of supporting information:

- Historical analogues from a curated database.
- Current context from domain-specific knowledge-base files.

This demonstrates retrieval as an analytics capability, not as a chatbot
feature.

## Decision Support

The output is designed to help analysts identify what matters, what resembles
prior cases, what differs, and what requires monitoring.

## Workflow Design

The project decomposes the analyst workflow into distinct stages. Each stage has
a clear input, output, and validation path.

## Analytics Product Thinking

The V2 Analyst Workbench turns the pipeline into a usable product surface. It
supports input, review, evidence tracing, and export while maintaining clear
boundaries against forecasting and investment advice.
