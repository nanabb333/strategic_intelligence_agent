## Coverage Assessment

V10 broadens the knowledge base from a small demonstration dataset into a more credible educational case library. The strongest coverage is now export controls, industrial policy, supply chain disruption, sanctions, regulatory action, geopolitical escalation, and technology competition.

## Remaining Dataset Limitations

- The records are still simplified educational summaries.
- Source URLs are intentionally not fabricated.
- Primary-source citation work remains a future improvement.
- Mechanism and outcome distributions are approximate audit categories.
- The dataset supports decision support and portfolio demonstration; it is not a real-world accuracy benchmark.
