# Demo Scenarios

These scenarios are designed for polished end-to-end portfolio review. They are examples of reviewer workflows, not forecasts or advice.

## Federal Reserve Rate Hike

Workflow:

1. Add evidence on central bank statement language, inflation data, employment data, market expectations, and company exposure.
2. Review Evidence Intelligence for source concentration and freshness.
3. Inspect Decision Readiness for macro, liquidity, and timing evidence gaps.
4. Run Domain Decision Evaluation for asset allocation and credit risk framing where relevant.
5. Compare Decision Pathway Drafts such as staged commitment, delay or wait, and contingency preparation.
6. Use Pathway Comparison Matrix for categorical trade-off review.
7. Record Decision Review notes and unresolved questions.

## Export Controls

Workflow:

1. Add official policy documents, company exposure notes, regulatory commentary, and supply-chain evidence.
2. Review duplicates, potential conflicts, and legal/compliance source coverage.
3. Inspect readiness gaps around licensing, customer exposure, operational constraints, and historical support.
4. Generate pathways including seek regulatory clarity, staged commitment, and contingency preparation.
5. Compare constraints, reversibility, execution complexity, and evidence quality.
6. Mark items needing compliance review in Decision Review.

## Supply Chain Disruption

Workflow:

1. Add logistics, supplier, insurance, port, and customer impact evidence.
2. Review freshness and source diversity in Evidence Intelligence.
3. Inspect readiness for operational execution risk, counterparty risk, and unknowns.
4. Generate pathways such as diversify or hedge, staged commitment, and contingency preparation.
5. Compare timing sensitivity, historical support, and execution burden.
6. Record unresolved questions about supplier substitution and customer commitments.

## Insurance Company Review

Workflow:

1. Add underwriting, claims, reserves, reinsurance, regulatory capital, and market-condition evidence.
2. Review source credibility and potential conflicts.
3. Inspect readiness and Domain Decision Evaluation for insurance business concerns.
4. Generate pathway drafts without ranking or recommending.
5. Compare assumptions, unknowns, regulatory constraints, and evidence support.
6. Record reviewer notes for items requiring compliance or actuarial review.

## Strategic Partnership

Workflow:

1. Add partner diligence, market access, governance, regulatory, financial, and operational evidence.
2. Review novelty and source concentration.
3. Inspect readiness gaps around partner risk, constraints, execution complexity, and historical support.
4. Generate pathway drafts such as staged commitment, delay or wait, seek regulatory clarity, and contingency preparation.
5. Compare pathways by reversibility, timing sensitivity, evidence support, and unresolved unknowns.
6. Record reviewer questions before any decision record is created.
