# Intelligence Reasoning Framework

## Purpose

V4 adds an intelligence reasoning framework on top of routing and retrieval.
The goal is not to predict outcomes. The goal is to help analysts compare
multiple plausible interpretations of the same event.

## Components

- Mechanism detection.
- Multi-lens analysis.
- Evidence assessment.
- Historical response patterns.
- Cross-domain lessons.
- Monitoring considerations.

## Why Multiple Interpretations Matter

Strategic events often have more than one valid interpretation. Export controls
can be read as technology containment, market access restriction, alliance
coordination, or supply chain pressure. A single summary can hide those
differences.

V4 makes competing interpretations visible so decision-makers can inspect what
evidence supports each lens and what evidence remains missing.

## Product Boundary

The framework avoids forecasts, probabilities, investment advice, and trading
recommendations. It uses language such as "One possible interpretation is" and
"Decision-makers may wish to monitor".
