## What Happened After

- The practical impact often moved into compliance operations, product planning, and supply-chain qualification.

## Market Expectations vs Actual Outcomes

- **Initial / mainstream expectation:** observers often expected immediate shipment disruption.
- **Market or user behavior:** local dataset does not contain enough market-outcome evidence for price or investor-gain claims.
- **Actual observed outcome:** organizations adapted by redesigning screening, sourcing, and planning routines.
- **Expectation gap:** the lasting work was often operational rather than only legal.
- **Lesson:** monitor implementation details, license decisions, and supplier qualification.

## What This Means Now
