# Product Naming Review

This sprint does not rename the product automatically.

## Current Name

Strategic Intelligence Decision Companion

### Strengths

- Accurate for strategic decision workflows.
- Preserves continuity with existing README, docs, dashboard title, tests, and portfolio material.
- Signals decision support rather than autonomous action.

### Weaknesses

- Longer than ideal for enterprise packaging.
- "Companion" can sound softer than the current mature platform capability.

## Alternatives Reviewed

| Candidate | Assessment |
| --- | --- |
| Enterprise Decision Intelligence Companion | Strong enterprise signal, but longer and less distinct. |
| Decision Intelligence Companion | Clean and concise, but loses the strategic-intelligence positioning. |
| Strategic Intelligence Decision Companion | Best continuity and still accurate after consolidation. |

## Recommendation

Keep `Strategic Intelligence Decision Companion` for the current portfolio release. Use `Enterprise Decision Intelligence Platform` as the positioning line, not the product name.
